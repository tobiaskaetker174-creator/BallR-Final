22. Community Champion Award — Fairness Recognition
Feature:

Monthly award for players with highest Fairness scores. Winners get free games + community recognition.
Details:

The Award: Based on existing Fairness-Score (not new votes):

Rankings:

    🥇 1st Place: 3 free games
    🥈 2nd Place: 2 free games
    🥉 3rd Place: 1 free game
    Top 10% (Fairness Score ≥95): 1 free game each

How It Works:

    Every player has a Fairness-Score (already tracked)
    At end of month, system ranks ALL players by Fairness-Score
    Top 3 get ranked rewards
    Additionally, ALL players in top 10% (score ≥95) get 1 free game

Anti-Gaming Safeguards: (Fairness-Score system already has anti-gaming measures built in — see Mirror Score #3 and Fairness Economy #7 for details)

Prevention Measures:

    Score Integrity: Fairness-Score calculated from anonymous peer ratings (hard to game)
    Minimum Threshold:
        Must have played minimum 10 games in the month
        Minimum 25 unique opponents (prevents friend-only rating cycles)
    Suspicious Pattern Detection:
        Algorithm flags if fairness score suddenly spikes
        Manual review if spike is >0.8 points in <7 days
    Recency Weight: Only games from last 30 days count
    Community Reporting: Players can report suspected gaming
        If 3+ reports → investigation
        Confirmed cheaters lose 20 fairness points + can't get award that month

Community Recognition:

    Winner's profile gets "Community Champion 🏆" badge (limited to current month)
    Featured on leaderboard + website "Hall of Fame"
    Optional: Winner gets social media feature ("Meet the Champion")
    No name requirement — can be anonymous if preferred

Why This Works:

    Rewards fairness explicitly (not hidden in Elo system)
    Creates monthly "success story" for good behavior
    Anti-cheating measures are strong but not obvious
    Ensures only truly fair players win (not just well-liked)
    Motivates new players: "Hey, I can be a Champion if I play fair"
